Project plan

  1. Conduct an expert review of the website and identify all the UX issues.
  2. Write down a list of requirements for the redesign based on all the found issues.
  3. Workshop a potential design for the home page, trying out multiple fonts, colours and layouts.
  4. Create a style tile based on the final design to aid during the further redesign.
  5. Create and finalise the full redesign.

Analysis and Planning

  • Conducted a detailed expert review of the existing webshop, identifying issues in navigation, layout consistency and item categorization.
  • Developed a persona to guide design decisions around cultural connection, and family engagement.
  • Defined requirements including: modernized style, coherent navigation, clear signposting, improved product pages, better categorization, and redesigned registration flow.

Ideation and Style

  • Iterated on homepage design concepts to establish a coherent visual style, implementing a red/blue palette based on the Dutch flag with accessibility and clarity in mind.
  • Produced a style tile documenting colors, typography, and interactive elements to ensure design consistency.

UI Design

  • Designed Figma wireframes and a navigation flow for key pages: homepage, categories, product page, registration.
  • Introduced a clear navigation hierarchy, simplified categories and signposting for intuitive navigation.
  • Redesigned product pages with persuasive layouts (large imagery, "add-to-cart" call to action, related products).
  • Improved registration flow by introducing separate form-filling steps and adding error-proofing to make the experience less overwhelming.
  • Delivered a fully-featured hi-fi prototype.
